About Me

Who I Am

My name is Noah Al Hadidi, and I am an Accessibility Subject Matter Expert (SME) with over 12 years of experience in **web accessibility, inclusive design, and assistive technologies**. As a blind professional, I bring **a lived perspective** into my work, ensuring that digital experiences are truly accessible.

My Journey

I hold a **Master's degree in Computer Information Systems** from **Colorado State University**, where I was the first blind Omani to earn a science degree. Despite the challenges of navigating a sighted world, I have built a career that bridges **technology and accessibility**.
