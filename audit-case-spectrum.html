Spectrum TC Accessibility Audit

Overview

This case study covers the accessibility audit performed on the Spectrum TC platform, focusing on WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.

Findings

  • Issues with keyboard navigation in forms and modal dialogs.
  • Insufficient color contrast in multiple sections.
  • Missing ARIA attributes for dynamic components.

Solutions Implemented

  • Added proper focus management for modal dialogs.
  • Improved color contrast to meet WCAG 2.1 AA requirements.
  • Implemented ARIA roles and attributes for assistive technology support.
